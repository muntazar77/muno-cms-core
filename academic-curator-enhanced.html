FAQ

Questions students usually ask before making a move.

What is the Dual Ausbildung system? expand_more

The Dual Ausbildung system combines school-based learning with paid company training. It is one of Germany’s strongest routes for people who want practical experience and a structured career start.

Do I need German fluency before applying? expand_more

For many Ausbildung and undergraduate paths, B1 or B2 German is important. Some postgraduate programs are available in English, but language expectations vary a lot by route and institution.

How long does visa processing usually take? expand_more

The answer depends on the embassy and country, but a realistic planning window is often between 4 and 12 weeks after a complete file is submitted. Starting early matters.

Can you review my documents before I apply? expand_more

Yes. Document review is one of the most valuable parts of the service because weak formatting, missing items, or poor positioning can delay everything.
